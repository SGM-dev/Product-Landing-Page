Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can a smartwatch connect to an Android phone or an iPhone?

    Yes, most (not all) smartwatches will connect to either an Android phone OR an iPhone. But not both at the same time.

  • Can you change the watch face on a smartwatch?

    Yes, and there are several watch faces to choose from. You can do it directly on your watch or from your connected phone.

  • Can I upgrade my watch to Wear OS?

    If your watch isn't already running WEAR OS, then you cannot upgrade to the newest version of WEAR OS. You cannot change your watch from using a Samsung OS to Wear OS, or from an Android OS to WEAR OS, etc.
